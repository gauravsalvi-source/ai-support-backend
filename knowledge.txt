REQUIRED RESPONSE:

Spreadr is an affiliate marketing app that allows merchants to import Amazon products into Shopify and redirect customers to Amazon using affiliate links.

Merchants can earn commission through the Amazon Associates Program when customers purchase products through their affiliate links.

Uninstall Steps
========================


How do I uninstall App?

Go to your Shopify admin.
Go to Apps. Click on the Apps and sales channel settings.
A pop-up window will appear displaying a list of all installed apps—locate the app you want to remove, click the three dots (···) next to it, and select Uninstall from the dropdown menu.
That's it! The app is now uninstalled. 

For detailed instructions, please refer to the Shopify guide on uninstalling apps.

https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/apps/uninstalling-apps#uninstall-app
