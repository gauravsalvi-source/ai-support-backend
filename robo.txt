TITLE:
How It Works?

TERMINOLOGY:
robo
robo importer
robo product import
robo shopify import
robo extension
robo how it works

REQUIRED RESPONSE:

Robo Product Importer allows merchants to import products into Shopify using product URLs or the browser extension.

The app can import various product details including:

* Product title
* Description
* Images
* Pricing

Merchants can review and edit product details before importing them into their Shopify store.

The app also supports bulk importing, helping merchants quickly add multiple products to their store.

Once imported, the products become regular Shopify products that can be managed directly from the Shopify admin.

For detailed instructions, please refer to:
https://robo-product-importer.tawk.help/article/how-it-works

TITLE:
What Is Markup Pricing?

TERMINOLOGY:
robo markup pricing
robo markup
robo pricing rule
robo price increase
robo product pricing

REQUIRED RESPONSE:

Markup pricing allows merchants to automatically increase the product price during the import process.

Using markup rules, merchants can:

* Increase product prices by a fixed amount
* Increase prices by a percentage
* Set custom pricing strategies for imported products

This helps merchants maintain profit margins without manually editing product prices after import.

For detailed information, please refer to:
https://robo-product-importer.tawk.help/article/what-is-markup-pricing
