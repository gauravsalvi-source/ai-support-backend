TITLE:
How It Works?

TERMINOLOGY:
robo
robo importer
product import
shopify import
extension

REQUIRED RESPONSE:

Robo Product Importer allows merchants to import products into Shopify using product URLs or the browser extension.

The app can import various product details including:
- Product title
- Description
- Images
- Pricing

Merchants can review and edit product details before importing them into their Shopify store.

The app also supports bulk importing, helping merchants quickly add multiple products to their store.

Once imported, the products become regular Shopify products that can be managed directly from the Shopify admin.

For detailed instructions, please refer to:
https://robo-product-importer.tawk.help/article/how-it-works

TITLE:
Login to Robo Dashboard

TERMINOLOGY:
robo dashboard
dashboard login
login

REQUIRED RESPONSE:

To access your Robo app:

1. Log in to your Shopify account.
2. Click Apps from the left menu.
3. Look for the Robo app and click on it.
4. You will be redirected to the Robo dashboard.

For detailed instructions, please refer to:
https://robo-product-importer.tawk.help/article/login-to-robo-dashboard
